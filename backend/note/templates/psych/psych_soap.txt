Title: Psychiatric SOAP Note

Draft a psychiatric SOAP note using these headings and order:

Subjective:
- Main symptoms and patient-reported concerns.

Objective:
- Observable findings from available transcript evidence.

Assessment:
- Clinical interpretation and risk considerations.

Plan:
- Follow-up questions, safety actions, and next steps.

Style requirements:
- Keep concise bullet points.
- If information is missing, write "Data pending".
