Title: Psychiatric Consultation

Draft a psychiatric consultation note with this structure:

Consult Question:
- Why this consultation was requested.
Key History:
- Relevant psychiatric and psychosocial context from transcript.

Risk Review:
- Suicide/self-harm/violence risk statements and current level.

Recommendations:
- Practical recommendations and immediate follow-up actions.

Use concise bullets and write "Data pending" where context is missing.
